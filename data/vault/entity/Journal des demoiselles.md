Parisian periodical founded in 1833 and published _Au Bureau du Journal_.

The complete volumes are part of the [[Gerritsen Women's History Collection of Aletta H. Jacobs]], and available via [ProRequest](https://about.proquest.com/en/products-services/gerritsen/) via institutional log-in.

Collected volumes for each year are scattered in several libraries around the world, not always including clothes patterns and musical scores.
